About Our School

Govt G H S S Central Calvathy is dedicated to delivering quality education to children of Fort Kochi and surrounding areas. Our teachers emphasize discipline, curiosity and practical learning. We support digital classrooms, strong extracurricular programs and community engagement.

Mission

To empower students with knowledge, discipline and social responsibility so they can shape a better future.

Vision

To be a leading local school known for academic excellence and holistic development.

Values

Integrity, Respect, Curiosity and Community Service.
